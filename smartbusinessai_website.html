About

Built From Real Experience.
Not Theory.

Every framework in this toolkit came from a real situation. A cash crisis in West Africa where the bank account was frozen overnight and the team had 72 hours to respond. A board meeting where 90 seconds was all there was to explain a $15 million variance. A PE due diligence where the accounts told a difficult story and the valuation was on the line.

Satyabrata Das spent 29 years as CFO and CEO across 18 countries, managing finance for 56 entities under NSE listing obligations. Finance in markets where the rules changed faster than the contracts. Where currency crises were not hypothetical. Where board credibility was earned in real rooms with real consequences.

These tools are not built from a textbook. They are built from what actually works when the stakes are real and the clock is running.
